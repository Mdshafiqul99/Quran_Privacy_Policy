Privacy Policy for Learn Quran App

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Your privacy — and the privacy of children who use our app — is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the "Learning Quran" app ("we", "our", "the app") collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you and your child's parent or guardian.

In short: The Learn Quran app does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to enter any personal information. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. The only data processing involved comes from showing advertisements through Google AdMob, which is described in detail below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or request any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or photos. You can use all learning content in the app without signing up or logging in.

To keep the app free, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may automatically collect limited technical and non-personal information, which can include:

— Device identifiers and advertising ID
— Approximate (non-precise) location, based on IP address
— Device type, operating system, and language settings
— App usage data, such as which ads were shown or clicked

This information is collected and processed by Google, not by us directly, and is used to deliver and measure advertisements.

2. Children's Privacy

Learning Quran is designed to be suitable for users of all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of young users in line with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy requirements.

— We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
— We do not require sign-up, login, or any personal details to use the app.
— Advertisements shown to children through this app are configured to use non-personalized ads and are served using AdMob's child-directed treatment settings, which limit the use of behavioral advertising and persistent identifiers for ad personalization.
— We do not knowingly allow children to make in-app purchases or to interact with third-party services that collect personal data, beyond the limited ad-serving technology described above.

If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided personal information through this app in a way that concerns them, please contact us using the details in Section 7 so we can investigate and assist.

3. App Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

— Internet access — required to load Quranic content (text, audio, or translations) and to display advertisements.
— Storage access (if applicable) — may be used only to allow offline caching of learning content for a smoother experience.

We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, call logs, or other private files on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service in our app:

Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. You can review Google's privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy, and learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use their services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal information on our own servers, there is minimal risk associated with our direct data handling. Any data processed through AdMob is handled under Google's own security and privacy safeguards.

6. Your Consent

By using the Learning Quran app, you (or, if you are a parent or guardian installing the app for a child) consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
